Edits persist in your browser and a ● marks any row you've overridden. Click ✕ to drop a paper from the total or ＋ to put it back.

Privacy: The page sends only your ORCID iD and chosen settings to Jamie's Apps Script (running on Google's infrastructure). No data is stored on this server. Your ORCID and last settings are remembered in your own browser via localStorage.

Caveat: Estimates are indicative. Real LOM allocation depends on Charité's faculty-board-approved share table (FBA), JIF currency, and RCR availability. Use this for planning, not for budgeting.
